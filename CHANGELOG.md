## [Unreleased]

### Added
- Initial release
- Global hotkey (Option + Space) for voice dictation
- Three shortcut modes: Auto, Toggle, Hold
- Streaming transcription with live preview
- Support for 10 Whisper models (tiny through large-v3)
- 13+ language support with auto-detect
- Menu bar integration with status indicator
- Floating recording indicator (Pill and Minimal styles)
- Multiple output modes (paste, clipboard, history)
- Transcription history (last 50 recordings)
- Guided onboarding with permissions setup and model download
- Settings for hotkey, model, language, appearance, and behavior
